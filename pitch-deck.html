The Problem

The people you rely on
aren't always reachable.

Filing taxes means chasing each other for documents. A medical emergency means a family breaking into a phone to find an insurance policy. When someone passes, heirs scramble for accounts they can't find.

  • Personal data today is all-or-nothing — locked in one head or fully public.
  • Nothing maps to how relationships actually work.
  • People cope by repeating themselves, endless back-and-forth, or breaking in.
The Solution

Mini-Me — a personal AI
agent trained on you.

Upload your bios, journals, documents, and Q&As. Mini-Me indexes them and answers as you would. Share one link; the people who matter can ask your agent anything they're allowed to — asynchronously, anytime.

Trained on real data

Your stories, knowledge, and personality — not a generic chatbot.

Answers as you

RAG pipeline draws only from what you've shared at each access level.

On your terms

You decide who sees what. Always.
